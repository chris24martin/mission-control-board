## 1) Access, Driveway, and Plowability

### Quick Ask List (script)
- “How old is the roof and when was it last replaced?”
- “Septic: when was it last pumped/inspected? Any repair history?”
- “Well: any recent water test? Any treatment system?”
- “Any basement water issues in spring thaw/heavy rain?”
- “Who plows the driveway and what does it cost?”
- “What internet options are actually available at the address?”
- “Any seasonal noise (ATVs, hunting, farms)?”
